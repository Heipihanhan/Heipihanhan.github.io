How efficient countries were in adopting EITI initiative, from announcing to candidacy to compliance. We calculate the distance between these three phases.

While there isn't a comprehensive way to accurately measure the news intensity about Syrian refugee from countries around the world, the GDELT Country Timeline data of news article mentions does shed some light on this issue, and we find significant differences between countries and continents.

The top 10 countries mostly associated with Syrian refugees in the news consist of 6 Middle Eastern countries and 4 European countries. After we compare the news intensity they received from the media with the number of refugees and asylum seekers they took, we discovered something interesting and have come to a better understanding regarding the "Words vs. Actions" concept in state response to the largest population movement in the 21st Century.
